Welcome to Wish Cycle! Here is a rundown of how to use the website:

Have an item you are wishing you could recycle but not sure how? Great! We're here to help. Follow these step by step insturctions:

  • Look at your item. If it is new don't recycle! Use it, list on your favorite website for free pick up, or donate to your local Goodwill.
  • If your item is used, you can do one of the following:
    1. Type the name of your item into the search box, click the "Submit" button. Recyling details are then displayed.
    2. If you are unsure of what your item is, click the "Search by picture" button.
      • If this is the chosen route, you will be taken to the next page where you can choose which category your item might fall under.
      • Click the category for your item.
      • You are then taken to the next page where you can select your item.
      • Recycling info with instructions will be displayed.
    3. If you know your item's recycling code, click the "Search by recycling code" button. Info of how to recycle your item will be displayed.

Thank you for doing your part to build a more sustainable future!
